# Product Definition - PastePaw

## Initial Concept
A beautiful, high-performance clipboard history manager for Windows and macOS. It aims to provide a local-first, private, and feature-rich experience using Rust (Tauri) and React.

## Target Audience
- **General Users:** Individuals looking for a simple, beautiful, and private way to manage clipboard history on Windows and macOS.
- **Developers & Power Users:** Users who benefit from advanced features like code explanation, terminal-friendly pasting (Shift+Insert), and AI integration.
- **Creative Professionals:** Designers and writers who manage large volumes of visual and text snippets and need organized folder structures.

## Core Goals & Success Factors
- **Performance & Speed:** Achieving near-instantaneous window toggling and fluid scrolling through unlimited history.
- **Privacy & Security:** Maintaining a strict local-only data policy and robust filtering for sensitive applications (e.g., password managers).
- **Visual Elegance:** Providing a modern, polished UI with seamless light/dark mode transitions that feels native to both Windows and macOS.
- **Ease of Use:** Ensuring a zero-friction user experience where managing history feels natural and intuitive.
- **AI-Enhanced Workflow:** Strategically integrating AI to summarize, translate, and explain content without compromising the local-first philosophy.

## Strategic Priorities
- **Cross-Platform Parity & Stability:** Merging and validating extensive macOS features to ensure complete compatibility and stability alongside the Windows version.
- **App Store Readiness:** Preparing the application for submission to Apple's App Store, including necessary refinements and compliance checks.
- **Feature Completion:** Implementing high-priority enhancements like advanced search filters (type, date, folder) to provide a comprehensive management tool.
